Our team members
Meet the dedicated team behind Euphoric Circus, each playing a vital role in our mission. From instructors teaching circus arts to mentors offering guidance, every member is committed to empowering youth. Discover the diverse talents driving our vision for a brighter future. Our young leadership program is a training scheme in which 15+ year olds can learn functional and transferable skills to help them into employment and further education. From skills such as project planning and event management to health and safety and first aid, our trainee scheme is free and provides young people with valuable work experience that they can take with them anywhere.
